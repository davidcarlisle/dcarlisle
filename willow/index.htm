Location

Souldern is roughly half way between Bicester and Banbury, three miles from Junction 10 of the M40.

Directions

From M40: Leave at Junction 10, take A43 Northeast towards Northampton for about 1/2 mile. At roundabout turn left on B4100 signed towards Ayhno. (This is the old A41.) After 2 miles turn left at the Bear Inn into Souldern village.

At the village pond turn sharp left into `Back Lane'. Keep straight on past the Chapel, and over a small rise. At the bottom, just before the road turns sharp right Willow House is on your left, by a large tree! See pictures below.
